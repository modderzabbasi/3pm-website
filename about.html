3P Investment Managers was founded in September 2022. We are a young company with decades of experience and strong credentials. The 19-member team is a healthy blend of experienced and young professionals.

Investment team with best in class team credentials

We incorporate a time-tested and remarkably successful investment talent. Prashant Jain, is our Founder and Chief Investment Officer. Prior to setting up 3P, Prashant was Executive Director and Chief Investment Officer at one of India's leading Asset Management Company, HDFC AMC, for 17 years. He has 32 years of experience in Indian equities, all of it with mutual funds. He has an unbroken track record of managing the same fund for over 28 years. This fund, HDFC Balanced Advantage Fund, delivered a CAGR of ~17.9% vs Sensex CAGR of ~9.6% from Jan 1994 - July 2022. Total AUM of HDFC AMC as on July 2022 was ~₹4,20,000 crores (US$ 50bn)

Ashwani Kumar is Portfolio Strategist and Co-Investment Manager at 3P. He has 28 years of experience in the investment management. He worked with Nippon Asset Management Company as a Senior Portfolio Manager for 17 years before joining 3P.

The investment team also comprises of 8 Research Analysts. They have experience ranging from 4-14 years across large and small organisations.

Our dealer Vishtaspa N. Rana has 18 years of experience with Tata Mutual Fund.

Operations and Risk Management

Sharad Mohnot is the Chief Compliance Officer at 3P. He has 19 years of experience in the field of Risk Management all of it with mutual funds. He was Chief Risk Officer at HDFC AMC for 10 years before joining 3P. He heads the Risk, Operations and Client servicing team of total 6 members at 3P.

Key People

Prashant Jain

Founder & Chief Investment Officer

Prashant Jain has 32 years of experience in Indian equities, all of it with mutual funds. He is a BTech from IIT-Kanpur and PGDM from IIM-Bangalore. He started his career with SBI Mutual Fund (1991-1993). He then moved to set up 20th Century Mutual Fund that became Zurich India Mutual Fund which was finally acquired by HDFC Mutual Fund in 2003. He thus has an unbroken track record of managing the same fund for over 28 years. This fund, HDFC Balanced Advantage Fund, delivered a CAGR of ~17.9% vs Sensex CAGR of ~9.6% from Jan 1994 - July 2022.

His key winning themes over these three decades were technology in 90’s, old economy from early 2000 to pre-Lehman, consumer and pharma from pre-Lehman to mid-2010’s and corporate banks, energy, defense, utilities etc. in the last phase. He also successfully called the tech bubble and avoided the excesses of pre-Lehman in infra, power and more recently in new age tech stocks. Attached note “SAAR” chronicles his three-decade journey.

Ashwani Kumar

Portfolio Strategist and Co-Investment Manager

Our Portfolio Strategist and Co-Investment Manager Ashwani Kumar, is another long-term professional with nearly 28 years of research and fund management experience. Ashwani Kumar started managing Reliance Vision Fund in 2003. He was one of the key members of the team at Reliance Mutual Fund (now Nippon India Mutual Fund) that scaled up the assets from ₹200 crores (US$ 24mn) in June 2003 to ~₹90,000 crores (US$ 11bn) in Feb 2020. He worked with Nippon Asset Management Company for 17 years before joining 3P.

Sharad Mohnot

Chief Compliance Officer

Sharad Mohnot is the Chief Compliance Officer at 3P. He has ~19 years of experience, all of it with the mutual fund industry. His last role was with HDFC Asset Management Company where he was Chief Risk Officer for ~10 years. He will bring in best practices in compliance, risk management and operations at 3P. He heads the Risk, Operations and Client servicing team at 3P which includes 2 chartered accountants and 1 company secretary.
