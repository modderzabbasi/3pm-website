Investment team with a proven track record

We incorporate a time-tested and remarkably successful investment talent. Our founder and Chief Investment Officer is Prashant Jain whose long investment track record is legendary.

Our Co-Investment Manager Ashwani Kumar has close to 3 decades of experience in investment management. The investment team also has 7 research analysts with experience ranging from 4-14 years. The team is a healthy blend of experienced leaders from large organisations and juniors to provide a younger perspective.

Key People

Prashant Jain

Founder & Chief Investment Officer

Prashant was Executive Director and Chief Investment Officer at HDFC Asset Management Company for a long stint till recently, before setting up 3P. He is the only fund manager in India, and one of the few globally, to have managed a large fund continuously for more than 28 years. In this long span, he has steered investments across multiple business and market cycles with great success. The basis of this success has been avoidance of excesses in each cycle and focus on investing in sustainable and reasonably valued businesses. Through the usual ups and downs of markets, Prashant has backed his astute and well-researched investment decisions, even in the face of temporary underperformance; his convictions have been eventually vindicated, with handsome rewards to investors in the funds managed by him over the long term. Further, by successfully managing large active equity funds that grew to Rs. One Lakh Crores, he has dispelled the common notion that large size of an investment pool always hinders performance.

Ashwani Kumar

Portfolio Strategist and Co-Investment Manager

Our Co-Investment Manager Ashwani Kumar, is another long-term professional with nearly 27 years of research and fund management experience with Zurich India Mutual Fund and the Nippon Asset Management Company.

Sharad Mohnot

Chief Compliance Officer

He is Chief Compliance Officer at 3P, with 18 years of experience in the mutual fund industry will bring in best practices in compliance, risk management and operations.
