Our Maiden Offering: 3P India Equity Fund 1

Category: Cat III AIF

Tenure: Open-Ended Scheme

Inception date: 24 Mar' 23

Fund Manager: Mr. Prashant Jain and Mr. Ashwani Kumar

Presentation

Objective: The Fund would carry on the activity of a Category III AIF and would raise resources on a private placement basis to make investments in permissible securities / instruments in accordance with the Regulations. The Fund has been set up with the objective to achieve long term capital appreciation by investing in permissible securities/instruments in accordance with this Memorandum and the Regulations. Fund will predominantly invest in listed Indian equities.

Investment strategy: The fund aims to remain effectively diversified across key sectors. The Fund shall invest across market capitalisations primarily focussing on sustainable/ competitive businesses at reasonable valuations/ price. Reasonable valuation would mean a price justified by the business outlook and growth prospects of the investee company. Thus, the Fund would not refrain from paying higher valuations at entry point for companies with higher growth prospects and a long runway of growth.
